## CRITICAL SUCCESS FACTORS

### 1. Document Everything
Every process you do must be:
- Written down
- Automated where possible
- Transferable to others
- Packaged for Empire OS

### 2. Hire Slow, Fire Fast
Only hire when revenue supports it (3:1 rule)
For every $10k/month new hire, need $30k/month revenue

### 3. Keep Profit Margins High
Target margins:
- Year 1: 80%+ (just you)
- Year 2: 60%+ (small team)
- Year 3: 50%+ (growing team)
- Year 4-5: 40%+ (scale mode)

### 4. Bootstrap Discipline
- No office (remote only)
- No unnecessary tools
- Ruthlessly cut costs
- Every dollar goes to growth or profit

### 5. AI Leverage
- Never hire for tasks AI can do
- Constantly improve automation
- AI handles 80%, humans handle 20%
- This is your moat
