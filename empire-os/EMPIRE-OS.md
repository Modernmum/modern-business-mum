## Database Schema

All businesses share one Supabase database:

**Tables:**
- `clients` - All paying customers across all services
- `leads` - Prospects for all businesses
- `content` - Generated content (blogs, social, emails)
- `jobs` - Job board listings
- `campaigns` - Outreach campaigns
- `analytics` - Performance tracking
- `billing` - Stripe subscriptions

## The AI Engine

One Claude instance powers everything:
- Generates templates
- Writes blog posts
- Crafts outreach emails
- Creates social media posts
- Qualifies leads
- Responds to inquiries

## Automation Stack

**Product Creation:** AI creates templates, job listings, content

**Sales:** Stripe handles payments across all businesses
